\section{Overview}
\label{sec:method-overview}

The objective function is given in \cref{eq:method-loss}.

\begin{equation}
  \label{eq:method-loss}
  \mathcal{L}(\theta) = \frac{1}{n} \sum_{i=1}^{n} \ell(f_\theta(x_i), y_i)
\end{equation}

\Cref{eq:method-loss} is minimised over the parameters $\theta$.
